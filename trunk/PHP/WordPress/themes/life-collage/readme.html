A guide to Life Collage

Life Collage is all about you. It's about sharing your life with the world. About getting back to the basics of blogging. About having fun.

Life Collage is a child theme of the Hybrid WordPress theme, which means you must have it installed for this thing to work.

How to install Life Collage

Once you've installed Hybrid, follow these steps:

  1. Unzip the life-collage.zip file (you've probably already done this).
  2. Upload the life-collage folder to your /wp-content/themes directory.
  3. Go to Appearance > Themes in your WordPress admin.
  4. Activate the Life Collage theme.

Lifestream Template

The theme comes packaged with an extra page template for you to use called Lifestream.

The Lifestream page template integrates with the FlickrRSS plugin and gives you an additional widget area named Utility: Lifestream Template, which can be useful for adding feeds to other places like Twitter or del.icio.us.

Widgets

Life Collage is packaged with two additional widget sections (aside from the numerous ones already available).

Utility: Splash
Sits in the splash box below the header and navigation area.
Utility: Lifestream Template
Located below the main content but above the footer when using the Lifestream page template.

Theme Support & Community

The most authoritative sources for finding out how to use the theme or do some nifty stuff are the Life Collage and Hybrid theme tutorials. I'll constantly update both with tutorials and frequently asked questions.

We also have a vibrant community at the Theme Hybrid Support Forums. If you ever lose the link, you can find it right from your Hybrid Settings page in your WordPress dashboard.
